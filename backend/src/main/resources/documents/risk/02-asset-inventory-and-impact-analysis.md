## Asset Inventory and Impact Analysis (Guide)

### Asset inventory
- Asset types: people, processes, applications, data, infrastructure, cloud services, and third parties.
- For each asset, record: owner, location, data classification, criticality, and exposure points.
- Capture dependencies: which business services rely on which assets.

### Impact analysis
Assess impact across dimensions:
- Confidentiality impact (data disclosure, privacy, legal exposure)
- Integrity impact (data tampering, fraud, loss of trust)
- Availability impact (service outage, degraded performance)
- Safety impact (for OT/critical operations where applicable)
- Compliance impact (regulatory obligations, audit evidence requirements)
- Reputational impact

### Likelihood analysis
Likelihood typically considers:
- Threat capability and motivation
- Attack surface and exposure (internet-facing, privileged access, segmentation)
- Vulnerability presence and patch status
- Control effectiveness (prevent/detect/response/recovery)

### Qualitative risk level
- Use a consistent risk matrix (likelihood x impact) and document assumptions.
- When evidence is missing, clearly mark uncertainty and propose evidence collection steps.
